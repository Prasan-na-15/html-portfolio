The Best Movies According to Prasanna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shawshank redemption

One of the movies which gave meaning to the word redemption.....Showcasing the life of a victim who had to go through a hard time in jail.

Avengers:End game

Really cool sci-fi movie involving dynamic scenes and setups to showcase the fight between two biggest forces in the universe.

The pursuit of happiness

Great example that shows "Nothing can stop you from achieving unless its your own self"......Shows the Good,Bad and Ugly sides of life in a single movie
